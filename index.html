“We shall not defeat any of the infectious diseases that plague the developing world until we have also won the battle for safe drinking water, sanitation and basic health care”

The TIDEy Project is in collaboration with partners, a two-day Public Health campaign to 1000 female students of the University of Abuja to emphasize the importance of sanitation and hygiene and also screen for preventable diseases of public health importance and vaccinate against them.
